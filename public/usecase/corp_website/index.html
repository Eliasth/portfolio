Healthcare Mobile App Redesign

Improving patient engagement and appointment management for a regional healthcare provider.

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Category: Healthcare
  • Year: 2025

Overview

Project Overview:

Redesigned the patient mobile app to streamline booking, reminders, and access to telehealth services.

Context & Problem:

Existing app suffered from low patient adoption due to confusing navigation and fragmented communication channels.

Design Process

Research

Conducted stakeholder interviews and patient surveys to identify bottlenecks in the current appointment journey.

Ideation & Concept

Mapped patient personas and journey flows to generate alternative navigation patterns and notification strategies.

Design

Produced iterative wireframes and high-fidelity prototypes focusing on simplified scheduling and clear call-to-action elements.

Execution & Delivery

Implementation

Collaborated with the development team to build reusable UI components within the existing React Native codebase.

Testing & Validation

Ran moderated usability sessions and A/B tests on appointment reminders to validate improvements.

Final Outcome

Post-launch metrics showed a 35% increase in appointment confirmations and a 20% reduction in support tickets.

Key Insights

Clear guidance during onboarding and consistent terminology significantly improve patient confidence and app adoption.
