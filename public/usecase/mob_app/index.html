E-commerce Checkout Optimization

Reducing cart abandonment for a direct-to-consumer retail brand through a streamlined checkout experience.

  • Duration: 6 months
  • Category: B2B Website
  • Year: 2025

Overview

Project Overview:

Optimized the checkout funnel to minimize friction across web and mobile storefronts.

Context & Problem:

Analytics revealed a high drop-off between shipping and payment steps, largely due to complex forms and limited payment options.

Design Process

Research

Analyzed heatmaps, session recordings, and customer support logs to pinpoint usability pain points.

Ideation & Concept

Explored progressive disclosure patterns and alternative payment integration concepts through design sprints.

Design

Refined a modular component library and designed responsive layouts emphasizing clarity and trust signals.

Execution & Delivery

Implementation

Partnered with engineering to deploy incremental updates behind feature flags, enabling safe rollout and regression monitoring.

Testing & Validation

Implemented funnel analytics dashboards and ran multivariate tests on form layouts and CTAs.

Final Outcome

Checkout completion rates improved by 28%, and customer satisfaction scores rose by 15 points.

Key Insights

Transparent cost summaries and flexible payment choices play a critical role in maintaining momentum at checkout.
